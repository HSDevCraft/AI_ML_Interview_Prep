## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency > Intensity**: Better to study 6-8 hours daily than burn out
2. **Active Recall**: Don't just read, practice explaining concepts
3. **Implement from Scratch**: Deepens understanding significantly
4. **Track Progress**: Check off completed items daily
5. **Sleep Well**: Memory consolidation happens during sleep
6. **Stay Healthy**: Exercise, eat well, take breaks
7. **Mock Interviews**: Practice under realistic conditions
8. **Review Mistakes**: Learn from every wrong answer
